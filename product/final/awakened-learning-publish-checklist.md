Use this as the minimum checklist to open sales.

- [ ] Product name locked

- [ ] Final sales page copy approved

## Product Delivery
- [ ] Guide file finalized

- [ ] Product created in SamCart
- [ ] Price set

## QA
- [ ] Mobile readability checked

## Launch Readiness
- [ ] 3-5 promo posts/emails drafted

- [ ] Mission Control updated with launch status

## Open Sales Standard
You are ready to open sales when:
- the offer is understandable quickly

- delivery works
