# Awakened Learning: The AI-Powered Curiosity Kit

## Simple Launch Plan

## Goal

Launch a clean, useful, low-friction digital offer that validates demand for curiosity-led, AI-supported family learning.

Primary business goal:
- generate early sales
- test message-market fit
- establish the product as a strong entry offer into a broader Awakened Learning ecosystem

## Offer Strategy

### Recommended v1 offer
Launch the product as a simple low-ticket digital kit.

**Suggested price:** $29

Why:
- low enough for low-friction buying
- high enough to signal real value
- suitable for a polished toolkit rather than a throwaway printable

## Positioning Statement

A practical family learning toolkit for parents who want to turn curiosity into meaningful projects and experiences—with AI as support, not replacement.

## What to Launch First

Keep version 1 tight.

### MVP contents
- welcome / philosophy guide
- quick-start guide
- curiosity prompts
- AI prompt library
- interest-to-project planner
- structure-without-school guide
- reflection prompts
- family learning map

Avoid overbuilding before validation.

## Core Launch Message

You do not need to recreate school at home to help your child learn deeply.

Supporting ideas:
- structure without school
- curiosity as the engine of learning
- polymath-style connection-making
- AI as a parent support tool
- real-world, project-based, human-centered learning

## Launch Assets Needed

### Essential
1. Product files finalized
2. Cover / simple visual mockup
3. Sales page
4. Checkout page
5. 5–7 email or post-sized promotional assets
6. Simple thank-you / delivery flow

### Nice to have
- short founder video
- sample page preview PDF
- one visual showing school-at-home vs curiosity-led learning

## Audience Angle

Primary audience:
- curious parents
- homeschooling families
- worldschooling families
- parents dissatisfied with conventional schooling
- families interested in AI but wary of overuse

Messaging should emphasize family learning freedom, not institutional reform.

## 2-Week Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Prep (Days 1–5)
- finalize product structure and naming
- confirm MVP contents
- confirm final price
- finish product documents and design pass
- create product cover / mockup
- finalize sales page
- set up checkout and delivery
- create promo posts/emails
- create one sample preview asset
- test the customer journey end-to-end

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Days 6–10)
- announce to warm audience first
- share the core problem / philosophy
- tell the story behind the product
- invite first buyers and feedback
- watch for objections and confusion

### Phase 3: Tighten + Extend (Days 11–14)
- refine messaging from real feedback
- strengthen FAQ / objections
- improve delivery if needed
- prepare evergreen versions of promo assets

## Success Metrics

### First signal goals
- first sales
- clear message resonance
- people understand what the product is quickly
- low-friction buyer journey

### Useful early metrics
- landing page conversion
- product sales
- email signups
- replies / qualitative feedback
- questions that reveal confusion or strong resonance

## Strategic Note

This product is not the final business.
It is the first strong offer in the Awakened Learning ecosystem.

The purpose is to create:
- proof of demand
- audience trust
- a product asset
- a foundation for future offers
