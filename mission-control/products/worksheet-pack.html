Worksheet 7 — Role Model Study Page

Purpose

Help children learn through the lives of real people.

Prompts

  • Role model name:
  • What are they known for?
  • Why are we studying this person?
  • What qualities stand out?
  • What did they build / discover / create / explore?
  • What can we learn from how they lived or thought?
  • What questions do we have about them?
  • What does this connect to in our current learning?
