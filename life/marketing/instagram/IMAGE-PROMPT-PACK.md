# IMAGE PROMPT PACK — Curiosity Driven Learning

Use these for faceless Instagram posts. Brand direction:
- warm illustrated style
- cozy homeschool vibe
- no real people / no photorealism
- earthy tones
- square format
- soft natural light
- gentle, calm, exploratory mood
- no text overlay unless specifically needed
