## Personal post photo directions
### Post 4
Use the Sedona hiking photo with Jonah and Noa at the Outer Limits trail sign. Keep the full sign visible if possible, with enough red rocks and open desert in frame to communicate place. If cropping for Instagram, keep the kids centered and preserve the feeling of space and adventure. Avoid heavy filters. Let it feel real, warm, and lightly cinematic rather than polished.

### Post 5
Use a real family photo that feels candid and grounded rather than posed. Best options: hiking, exploring outdoors, walking together, or a relaxed travel/worldschooling moment with Jonah and Noa. Prioritize warmth, connection, and movement. Avoid overly polished portraits. The image should feel like real family life with curiosity and freedom in it.
