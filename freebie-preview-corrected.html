About

A simpler, more human way to learn at home

I’m Josh Dates — a former teacher and homeschool dad who cares deeply about helping families keep learning alive, connected, and meaningful. I built Curiosity-Driven Learning to give parents practical tools that support wonder, deeper thinking, and real-world learning without recreating school at home.

AI can be useful, but only when it stays in the right role. Around here, it supports the process — it doesn’t replace the relationship.
